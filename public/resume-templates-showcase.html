What you're looking at

  • open-resume reproduces references/open-resume/src/app/components/Resume/ResumePDF/* — the themed accent bar (themeColor), 20pt left-aligned colored name, summary line, and the icon+text flex contact row from ResumePDFProfile.tsx. Section headings inherit the theme color with an underline.
  • resume-lm reproduces references/resume-lm/src/components/resume/editor/preview/resume-pdf-document.tsx — centered 24pt bold Helvetica name, contact row with • separators, uppercase 10pt section titles with a thin #e5e7eb rule, and the dense bullet style with • dots.
  • Both pages target US Letter dimensions (8.5in × 11in) for a faithful preview of the printed/PDF result.

How to open

  • Run npm run dev in resuscore/ and visit http://localhost:3000/resume-templates-showcase.html.
  • Or just open the file directly in any browser — no JS, no build needed.
